Sync your planned routes from Mapy.cz directly to Garmin Connect as courses — one click, no manual exports.


Routomil bridges Mapy.cz and Garmin Connect so you can plan routes in your favorite Czech map app and ride or hike them with your Garmin device — without any manual downloading, converting, or uploading.


HOW IT WORKS
1. Plan a route on Mapy.cz
2. Open the Routomil popup
3. Select activity type (Cycling or Hiking)
4. Click "Sync to Garmin"
Your route appears in Garmin Connect, ready to send to your device.


KEY FEATURES

One-Click Sync
Transfers your Mapy.cz route to Garmin Connect as a course in seconds. No exports, no file management.

Folder Sync
Sync an entire Mapy.cz folder at once — all routes uploaded as individual courses in a single operation.

Elevation & Waypoints
Preserves elevation data and course points from your route, so your Garmin device shows accurate climb profiles.

Secure Garmin Login
Signs you in through the standard Garmin Connect browser flow, including full MFA/2FA support. Your password is never stored — only an encrypted session token is kept locally.

Activity Type Selection
Choose Cycling or Hiking per sync, or set a default in settings.

Sync History
The popup keeps a log of your recent syncs with direct links to each course in Garmin Connect.


PRIVACY & SECURITY
- No tracking, no analytics, no ads
- Session tokens encrypted with AES-GCM in local storage
- All communication over HTTPS
- CSRF protection on every Garmin API request
- Data never leaves your browser except to connect.garmin.com and mapy.cz
- Sensitive data cleared on logout


REQUIREMENTS
- A Mapy.cz account with saved routes
- A Garmin Connect account
- Google Chrome (Manifest V3)
